New Cosmic Dinner Entries Live

Today, five new Cosmic Dinner recipes hit the menu. From comfort noodle soup to teff injera bowls, these dishes blend global flavors with a focus on simplicity and nourishment. Each recipe is a small experiment in how food can feel both familiar and surprising. I’m still figuring out the rhythm of this food philosophy—balancing intention with flexibility.

Short Film Concept: Creativity Meets AI

I’m sketching a short film idea that asks, What if creativity had a conversation with AI? It’s not about robots replacing humans, but about how tools shape our thinking. The script is rough, but the question feels important. How do we stay human when the world keeps changing so fast?

Content Strategy: Stories Over Systems

Right now, I’m wrestling with what stories to share. Cosmic Dinner is more than recipes—it’s about connection, curiosity, and the messy process of making things. I want to focus on real, unpolished stories that feel like a conversation. No filters, no fake perfection. Just raw ideas and the people who make them.
